Ashok Leyland – History

Ashok Leyland was founded in 1948 and is one of India’s leading commercial vehicle manufacturers. The company is headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. It initially started as Ashok Motors and later collaborated with Leyland Motors of the UK.

Over the years, Ashok Leyland has played a major role in developing India’s transport and infrastructure sector by manufacturing trucks, buses, defence vehicles, and special purpose vehicles.
